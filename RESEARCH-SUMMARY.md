### Red Flags to Avoid
1. No photos or blurry/stock images
2. Prices significantly below market (theft indicator)
3. Seller vague about purchase date/invoice
4. Battery health unknown or evasive
5. No local inspection option
6. Urgent sale pressure ("need quick sale")
7. Missing charger without price reduction
8. Brand new accounts with multiple listings
9. No warranty documentation or serial verification
10. Generic descriptions lacking specs

### Green Flags (Safe Purchases)
1. Seller shows receipt/invoice
2. Multiple clear angle photos
3. Specific specs: storage, RAM, chip, battery %
4. Established business/long seller history
5. Willing to meet in safe public location
6. Original packaging/charger included
7. AppleCare+ remaining/transferable
8. Battery health 95%+
9. Honest about cosmetic damage
10. Price negotiable with justification

### Safety Protocol
1. Meet in well-lit public location (shopping centre)
2. Bring friend, notify someone of location
3. Test laptop fully before payment
4. Verify serial number on Apple.com
5. Check battery health (Coconut Battery app)
6. Confirm no Activation Lock
7. Get seller ID photo and contact details
8. Pay cash or Gumtree secure payment
9. Get written receipt with serial number
10. Verify not listed as stolen goods
